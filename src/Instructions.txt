JungleRun

Welcome to Jungle Run
Are you ready to run and avoid seemingly blocky objects?
well than this is the game for you

At any time the game can be ended by pressing the "x" key
While your monkey (the red circle on your screen) is running he can't stop.
Move up and down using the "up" and "down" directional keys
avoid the blue rectangles or your game is over!

Have fun!
